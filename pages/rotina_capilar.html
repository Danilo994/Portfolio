Rotina Capilar

Hair care routine tracker

About the project

I started growing my hair out, and during this process I struggled to learn how to properly take care of long hair. My biggest challenge was understanding how different product combinations affected my hair, and how to build and maintain a consistent hair care routine.

So, I decided to create a project where I can register the products I use, the types of washes, and keep track of my daily hair care steps. Later on, I implemented a rating system for each care entry, allowing me to analyze which routine worked best for me and delivered the results I liked the most.
